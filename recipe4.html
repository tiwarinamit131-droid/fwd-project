Butter Chicken with Garlic Naan

Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Servings: 4
Category: Non-Vegetarian

Ingredients

For Butter Chicken:

  • 800g boneless chicken thighs, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tablespoon kasuri methi
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

For Garlic Naan:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  • Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt for 30+ minutes.
  • Saute onions and ginger-garlic, then add tomato puree and chicken.
  • Simmer with water 20 minutes, then add butter, cream, and kasuri methi.
  • For naan, proof yeast, then knead dough and rest 1 hour.
  • Roll naan, cook on hot skillet, spread butter and coriander.
  • Serve butter chicken with garlic naan hot.

Chef's Tips

Pro Tip 1: Use full-fat cream and butter for rich, authentic butter chicken flavor.

Pro Tip 2: Let the marinated chicken sit for at least 30 minutes (or overnight) for deeper flavor.

Pro Tip 3: Cook naan on high heat and flip quickly for soft, charred edges.
