Moong Dal Halwa

️ Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 35 minutes
Servings: 4-5
Category: Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow moong dal, soaked for 1 hour and drained
  • 1 cup ghee
  • 3/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups whole milk (or water for lighter version)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 10-12 cashews, broken
  • 10-12 almonds, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons raisins
  • Chopped pistachios for garnish

Instructions

  • Roast nuts and raisins in ghee; set aside.
  • Roast moong dal until light golden and nutty.
  • Add milk and cook until dal softens and liquid reduces.
  • Add sugar and cardamom; cook until ghee separates.
  • Stir in nuts and raisins, garnish with pistachios.
  • Serve hot.

Chef's Tips

Pro Tip 1: Stir continuously while cooking halwa to avoid burning and achieve smooth texture.

Pro Tip 2: Use full-fat milk for richer taste and creamier consistency.

Pro Tip 3: Keep a little extra ghee warm to drizzle at the end for shine and richness.
