Poha

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty: Medium
Servings: 4
Category:Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha), rinsed and drained
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut (optional)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Rinse poha until soft and drain fully.
  • Saute mustard and cumin seeds with curry leaves, then onions and green chili.
  • Add potatoes with turmeric and chili, cook until tender.
  • Stir in poha, sugar, salt; warm for 2-3 minutes.
  • Finish with lemon juice, peanuts, coconut, and coriander.
  • Serve hot with tea or chaat masala.

Chef's Tips

Pro Tip 1: Rinsing poha until it softens makes it fluffy; avoid over-rinsing to prevent sogginess.

Pro Tip 2: Use roasted peanuts for crunch and a squeeze of lemon for freshness right at the end.

Pro Tip 3: For extra texture, add sev or crushed papdi when serving; it makes the dish restaurant-style.
